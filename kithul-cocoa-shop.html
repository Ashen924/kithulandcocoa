Our Story

More Than Just a Café

Kithul and Cocoa is not just about food — it's about experience. Our café will serve up bold, island-inspired meals alongside immersive community events and eco-conscious retail.

Rooted in the bold, vibrant flavors of Sri Lanka and inspired by global culinary traditions, we're creating a space where fresh ideas meet soulful ingredients — served beachside.

From smoky island spices to handcrafted tropical cocktails, every dish will be a celebration of local roots and worldwide inspiration.

Our journey began with a passion for Sri Lankan cuisine and a vision to share these flavors globally. Now, we're excited to bring our carefully crafted products directly to your home.
